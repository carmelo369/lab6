Welcome to Jupiter

Jupiter is the largest and massive outer planet of the Solar System. Jupiter is a gas giant and it has a solid inner core. Jupiter is mainly composed of hydrogen and helium. In addition, this planet has a Great Red Spot that is a gigantic hurricane. Finally, Jupiter has 75 moons, and the 4 largest moons are Io, Europa, Ganymede, and Callisto.
