Welcome to Mars

Mars is the last planet of the inner planets. This planet is also known as the red planet due to the color generated by iron oxide on its surface. In addition, Mars is a rocky planet and its surface is altered by volcanoes. In addition, Mars has two moons Phobos and Deimos. Moreover, many missions have been created to visit this planet in search of life and water.
